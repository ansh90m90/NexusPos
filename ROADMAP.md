# Roadmap for Nexus

We're excited about the future of Nexus! Here are some features we're planning to add.

## Short-Term (Next 3 Months)
- [ ] **Multi-Store Support**: Manage multiple stores from a single Nexus instance.
- [ ] **Advanced Reporting**: Add more charts and reports for deeper insights.
- [ ] **Customer Loyalty Program**: Reward customers for their purchases.
- [ ] **Integration with Payment Gateways**: Support for online payments.

## Mid-Term (Next 6 Months)
- [ ] **Mobile App**: A dedicated mobile app for Nexus.
- [ ] **Inventory Forecasting**: Predict stock levels based on historical data.
- [ ] **AI-Powered Insights**: Use AI to provide personalized recommendations.
- [ ] **Integration with E-commerce Platforms**: Sync Nexus with your online store.

## Long-Term (Next 12 Months)
- [ ] **Global Expansion**: Support for multiple languages and currencies.
- [ ] **Enterprise-Grade Features**: Add more features for larger businesses.
- [ ] **Integration with Third-Party Services**: Connect Nexus with your favorite tools.
- [ ] **Open API**: Allow developers to build their own integrations.

*Stay tuned for more updates!*
