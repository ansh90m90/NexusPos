# Support for Nexus

We're here to help you get the most out of Nexus! Here are some ways to get support.

## Documentation
- Check the [README.md](README.md) for basic information and installation instructions.
- Explore the code and comments for detailed information on how Nexus works.

## GitHub Issues
- Use the GitHub Issue Tracker to report bugs or suggest features.
- Provide as much detail as possible, including steps to reproduce the issue.
- Mention the version of the application and the browser you're using.

## Community Support
- Join our community on [Discord](https://discord.gg/your-discord-link) or [Slack](https://slack.com/your-slack-link) (if available).
- Ask questions and share your experiences with other Nexus users.

## Email Support
- Send an email to the Nexus Team for any questions or feedback.
- We'll do our best to respond to your inquiry within 48 hours.

Thank you for using Nexus!
